About Us

WOW Collective is a unique concept with a purpose that goes beyond business. We are a one stop solution for all fashion requirements viz. apparel, accessories and jewellery. We proudly present a vast portfolio of Basics, Fashion-forward collections, Occasion-wear, License wear, Promotional clothing, Uniforms and Inner-wear across Men, Women and Kids.

We are one of a kind B2B concept with a vision to be pioneers in the global marketplace with our unmatched products, quality standards, pricing and services. With a strong global sourcing and manufacturing network our product lines are especially customised to offer a WOW addition to the brick-and-mortar stores, online retailers and boutiques worldwide.

Working With Us

Our expertise enables us to have a very simplied way of working. It can be summarised as a Simple 4 Step Process.

Step 1.

Send us a detail description of your requirements or request an appointment with our team.

Step 2

Our team will process the feasibility of your order. After all of the aspects are validated, we shall present you our plan and quotation.

Step 3

Once the quotation is approved, your order’s first sample will be made and presented to you.

Step 4

After the sample approval, your order will be sent to production. As soon as your order is done, we shall arrange for the shipment.
